Define length, width, and height as the lengths on the x-axis, y-axis, and z-axis, respectively
All boards are axially aligned with the coordinate axes

Format description:
	plate [index]:	(length,width,height), 
			(The x-axis coordinates of the center of the plate, The y-axis coordinates of the center of the plate, The z-axis coordinates of the center of the plate)
plate 1:(1.0, 0.3, 0.06),
	(0.0, 0.25, 0.6)
plate 2:(1.0, 0.3, 0.06),
	(0.0, 0.25, 1.1)
plate 3:(1.0, 0.3, 0.06),
	(0.0, 0.25, 1.6)
plate 4:(0.06, 0.3, 1.0),
	(-0.5, 0.25, 1.1)
plate 5:(0.06, 0.3, 1.0),
	(0.0, 0.25, 1.1)
plate 6:(0.06, 0.3, 1.0),
	(0.5, 0.25, 1.1)
	
All coordinates are world coordinate system
